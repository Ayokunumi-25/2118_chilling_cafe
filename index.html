About our cafe

saragota Cafe is a simple HTML website, coupled with css and javascript. i added animations using scroll and view to give it a kind of ux feeling, though not the best but i gained more insight on how to make use of animation timeline and javascript on scroll function.

i learnt how to make use of animation range both for entry and exit when you scroll into the desired width. i also learnt how to make use of scaling in animation: The higher the scale the more the image tends to grow on scroll, i implemented this into the intro image. i also added shadow to the into image , change the background of the whole HTML ALSO.
